OUR DRIVER’S & DRIVER TRAINING

Our experienced drivers are well trained, professional and courteous. All of our drivers have a valid PDP license and must complete an extensive in-house program focusing on customer service and road safety. Our drivers are currently undergoing professional driving certification through TETA.. We aim to exceed your expectations with our professional service

FOR YOUR SAFETY - OUR TECHNOLOGY

We take your safety very seriously. All Eagle Taxi-cabs vehicles are in constant communication with our call centre, 24 hours a day;, 7 –days a week; our drivers are carefully screened before being employed and our vehicles are continuously tracked by Netstar GPS Systems – providing us with the positions of our vehicles every few minutes.

OUR CALL CENTRE STAFF

We are also extremely proud of our radio control staff – who will respond to your calls efficiently and professionally – no job is too short or too long – call or email us and we will get you to your destination.
